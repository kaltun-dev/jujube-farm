THE WAY WE FARM

We have three simple priorities. Look after our animals, look after the land, and produce good food. Our animals are grown slowly to maturity and you can taste the difference in our award-winning product.

It’s our firm belief that if the first two are done with integrity, that means truly caring for the welfare of our livestock and our precious soil – the third takes care of itself. We’ve been farming organically for over 20 years and it takes time and commitment to give back the land what we take out.

We offer a wide range of forever expanding varieties of citrus fruits, apples, pears, tropical fruits as well as soft fruits like blackberries, redcurrants, gooseberries, blueberries and raspberries.
